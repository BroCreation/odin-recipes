Biryani Chawal

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish that originated in South Asia, featuring rice, meat, and spices.

It's known for its aromatic flavors and complex taste, often incorporating ingredients like saffron, turmeric, and various spices. The rice is typically basmati and can be layered with marinated meat or vegetables, creating a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Basmati
  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Various Spices
    • turmeric
    • chili powder
    • garam masala
    • saffron

Steps

  1. Wash the basmati rice. Before you begin cooking, you need to wash the rice.
  2. Soak the rice. After you have you rinsed the rice, soak it
  3. Chop the vegetables. Make sure you wash the vegetables and set them aside, ready to be added to your rice.
  4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Add mint leaves, coriander leaves, and green chilies to the pan.
  6. Add garam masala, chili powder, carrots, peas and beans.
  7. Pour in 8 cups of water.
  8. Add the rice. Pour the rice into the boiling water
  9. Serve. Once the rice has cooked, serve the biryani hot.
