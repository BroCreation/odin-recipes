Biryani Chawal

Description

Daal Chawal is a comforting and humble dish made with boiled lentils served over steamed rice, popular across all regions of Pakistan.

It's praised for being nutritious, affordable, and easy to digest, often seasoned with a simple tempering of onions, garlic, and spices to enhance its earthy taste.

Ingredients

  • Masoor or Moong
  • Daal (Lentils)
  • Basmati Rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices
    • turmeric
    • red chili powder
    • cumin seeds
    • salt

Steps

  1. Rinse and soak lentils for 15-20 minutes.
  2. In a pot, add lentils, water, turmeric, salt, and boil until soft.
  3. In a separate pan, heat oil and saut√© chopped onions until golden.
  4. Add tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, and cumin seeds.
  5. Cook until oil separates and pour this tempering over the boiled lentils.
  6. Cook rice separately by boiling until tender, then drain excess water.
  7. Serve the hot daal over rice with pickles or salad.
  8. Optional: garnish with coriander and green chilies.
