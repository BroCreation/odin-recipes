Pulao Chawal

Pulao / Pilaf

Description

Pulao is a fragrant rice dish made with meat or vegetables and whole spices, commonly served in Pakistani homes.

It's known for its subtle yet rich flavor profile, with aromatic ingredients like bay leaves, cinnamon, and cloves, cooked in a meat or vegetable stock to give the rice depth and savoriness.

Ingredients

  • Basmati
  • Rice
  • Beef or Chicken
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Whole Spices
    • cumin seeds
    • bay leaves
    • cloves
    • cinnamon sticks

Steps

  1. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 20-30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, saut√© sliced onions in oil until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add meat, yogurt, and whole spices; cook until the meat is tender.
  5. Pour in water and bring it to a boil.
  6. Add the soaked rice and let it cook uncovered until the water evaporates slightly.
  7. Cover and simmer on low heat until rice is fully cooked and fluffy.
  8. Gently fluff with a fork and serve hot.
